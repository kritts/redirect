Redirect: Quickly navigate to any URL

Never lose a bookmark again. Redirect is a Chrome extension
that enables you to create custom keys to any URL.

Download Redirect

1. Open Redirect

Do you have a favorite mojito recipe? Or a Google Doc you often open? Rather than search through your lists of bookmarks or Google Drive folders, you can create a Redirect. That simple keyword will take you straight to your site and can save you clicks and time. It simplifies your Chrome workflow, allowing you to quickly get to your favorite sites.

2. Create a Redirect

Redirects can be any length and can include non-alphanumeric characters. This presents a number of interesting use cases. For example, you could set newdoc to map to docs.google.com/document/create, enabling you to create a new Google Doc with just a few characters.

3. Use your new Redirect

To trigger Redirect, type into the address bar ; (a semicolon), followed by a space. Next, type your saved Redirect and then enter. This will open the saved URL in your browser. You can view all of your saved Redirects under the Settings page. You can also delete Redirects and create new ones in the Settings page.
